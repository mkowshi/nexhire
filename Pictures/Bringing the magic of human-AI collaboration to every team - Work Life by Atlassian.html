Bringing the magic of human-AI collaboration to every team

Powerful Rovo search, chat, and agents for all

For over 20 years, Atlassian has been in the business of deeply understanding teamwork. Our customers trust us to know the ins and outs better than anyone. With our AI capabilities already redefining work for over a million users every month, weve seen with absolute certainty that the future of teamwork is human-AI collaboration.

Now, were making our enterprise AI solution, Rovo, available so that all of our customers can add the most advanced AI-powered search, chat, and agents to the team. We believe that unlocking the limitless potential of your own knowledge is fundamental, not a privilege you should have to pay extra for.

You get Rovo! And you get Rovo!

Thats right. Rovo has proven to be such a force for teamwork that were making sure everyone gets to experience the transformative power of enterprise search, chat, and agents (and then some!). We’re so excited for Jira, Confluence, and Jira Service Management users to begin teaming up with Rovo as part of their subscriptions in the coming monthsstarting with our Premium and Enterprise, with Standard customers to follow.

Because if we can no longer imagine our day-to-day without Rovo (fun fact: it is the highest-utilized AI tool reported by Atlassians), then you shouldnt have to either.

callout

All the goodness you’ll get with Rovo included in your existing subscription:

  • Rovo Search app: World-class enterprise search that’s personalized to youtapping your organization’s entire knowledge base including all your favorite connected SaaS apps
  • Rovo Chat app: An always-there AI work companion that’s ready to share important context, answer your questions tirelessly, and make suggestions tailored to your work
  • Rovo Agents: Out-of-the-box and custom-built specialized AI teammates ready to get to work alongside you
  • *New* Rovo Studio app: A brand new one-stop-shop where you can build agents, automations, assets, and more with or without code

Powered by the enterprise-grade Atlassian platform with its Teamwork Graph to connect your AI workflows and enterprise data across teams, goals, projects, and work.

Premium enterprise search that doesnt come at a premium

Our recent State of Teams survey found that knowledge workers spend a whopping 25% of their time just searching for answersthat’s 2.4 billion hours wasted every year! The good news is that Rovo Search is more connected than ever and indexes new data nearly instantly, giving it the breadth, depth, and personalization to solve this massive problem and a whole lot more.

  • Breadth that might take your breath away: With 50 connectors and counting across Atlassian and third parties (like Gmail, OneDrive, Notion, and more), app-specific knowledge siloes and context-switching become a thing of the past. Soon, you’ll even be able to build your own custom connectors for greater flexibility.
  • Revel in the relevance: Get hyper-relevant context from Atlassian and third-party links and a direct answer from a knowledge card customized for you at the top. Users are 60% more successful with Rovo Search than a leading open-source enterprise search engine.
  • Answers that make you feel truly understood: Rovo Search rises far above the pack with tailored results served straight from the rich context of Teamwork Graph to zero in on what really matters just to you.
  • AI innovation that respects user permissions: Most importantly, Search is built on the secure, enterprise-grade infrastructure of our platform and always respects the permissions of the content it shares.

With Rovo, our employees can seamlessly access global knowledge across Atlassian and non-Atlassian tools, like our Google suite. With teams across more than 28 countries and multiple time zones, Rovo accelerates information discovery and summarization, enabling employees to find answers in minutes without waiting for support. This efficiency is transforming how we collaborate and solve problems at scale.

Kasia Wakarecy, Vice President of Enterprise Apps & Data at Pythian

Everything you’ve wanted to learn is just a chat away

The Rovo Chat app is an always-there AI work companion and your fast track to happier teams, fewer meetings, and, dare we say, greater wisdom. Thats because context is always king with Chat; its woven deep into the fabric of your Atlassian apps and the tools you use every day to provide a contextual framework so that youre always clued in and engaged in a culture of learning.

We mean it when we say Chat is that teammate. Chat:

  • Makes itself completely indispensable: Chat can handle your most complex questions (with follow-ups!), retain context across discussions, and teach you something new. It can even take action on your behalf across your favorite Atlassian apps or beyond by sending a Slack message, creating a Google doc, creating an Outlook Calendar event, and more.
  • Safeguards your data: Rest assured that Chat is an enterprise-grade and secure conversational experience safely grounded in your enterprise knowledge that respects your permissions.
  • Goes one deeper: Get all your hyphenated superlatives ready because soon, Chat will get Deep Research. We’re talking comprehensive, high-quality content generation and super in-depth information gathering, all within mere minutes. Deep Research mucks right in to synthesize insights from across your organizational Teamwork Graph data and tailor them just for you.
  • Is always by your side: You can get Chat as a browser extension, integrated with your Atlassian apps, and now on mobile via the Confluence app. Developers can even stay in the flow by chatting with Rovo directly in their IDE with GitHub Copilot and Gemini Assist extensions.

One of the most basic, yet most impactful ways I use Rovo Chat in our tests is to clean up meeting notes and automatically turn them into tickets in Jira. A one-hour meeting used to turn into another hour of project management work with all the follow up. But with Rovo, this work can be done in 15 minutes, and the time I saved can be put toward more strategic work.

Fred Frenzel, Project Management Office Director, HarperCollins

The AI teammates we’ve all been waiting for

Sometimes, progress from a year in the fast-paced world of AI almost feels equal to that of a regular decade. Atlassian was one of the first to introduce agents a year ago, and now we cant remember what its like to operate a business without them! And were not the only ones. Nearly 2,000 Rovo Agents have already been integrated into customer workflows to reimagine what teamwork looks like for all teams.

Rovo Agents are changing the game for Atlassians and our customers alike. Theyre specialized AI-powered teammates who operate with defined goals, characters, knowledge, and actions to guide their behavior and tasks. They seamlessly address your real-world challenges and use specialized skills to move work forward, facilitating brainstorms, analyzing customer feedback, identifying knowledge gaps, and so much more. You can get them out-of-the-box or custom-build them based on your needs.

We’ve used agents to automate tedious tasks (like creating recaps and issues directly from meeting notes), draft documents (like product requirements and SOPs), and make more informed decisions (like generating reports that guide business and resourcing choices).Multiple times a day, I think to myself, I cant wait to use an agent for this. I feel that we’ve only scratched the surface with agents. The skys the limit!”

Fred Frenzel, Project Management Office Director, HarperCollins

Teamwork Collection Agents redefine how work gets done

Our newly unveiled Teamwork Collection is a curated combination of Jira, Confluence, and Loom, supercharged with powerful agents. Our favorite part? These out-of-the-box agents are purpose-built for all of your teams. For example, our Workflow Builder leverages natural language to design and build custom workflows for any process in Jira (statuses, transitions, rules, you name it) so that even the most complex workflows are easier to build for everyone on the team.

Dev AI agents help devs do more of what they love: code

Rovo Dev Agents, now available in Beta, target the 84% of time developers spend outside of coding so that developers can do more of what they love. These out-of-the-box agents specialize in understanding the entire development ecosystem, from business requirements to technical implementation. Take the Code Reviewer Agent, for example. Its an intelligent review companion that analyzes pull requests against technical requirements and business objectives, checking code changes against acceptance criteria in Jira, spotting potential issues before they reach production, and reducing review cycles.

Agents to improve service delivery across your entire organization

Whether it’s empowering teams to respond swiftly to service requests or streamlining incident management, service delivery-focused Rovo Agents are absolute pros at collaborating with humans to address one of the most important tasks of all: elevating the user experience. The Root Cause Analyzer (coming soon!) helps you pinpoint the cause of an incident and surfaces critical context, such as related deployments, PRs, commits, and Jira work items, to help responders quickly resolve it.

Introducing Rovo Studio: A seriously cool new app for AI builders

Builders are at the very heart of what makes teams tick. For many, many years, theyve been pushing the boundaries of what can be achieved with off-the-shelf solutions here at Atlassian, and in years to come, they will be essential to taking AI to the next level.

Were a group of builders ourselves. Weve built thousands of agents internally at Atlassian that have improved every corner of our business. Team members of all stripes, whether in legal, engineering, marketing, or leadership, have been writing their own agents to help them move away from doing the work of work and into real innovation and problem-solving.

We took everything weve learned and created the brand new Rovo Studio app as a one-stop-shop to:

  • Build custom AI-powered agents with low-code/no-code options
  • Tailor processes and rules with automation
  • Model real-world objects with assets and schemas
  • Curate rich, interactive views of content with hubs

The wizardry behind the scenes of Rovo

AI the Atlassian way is a magical cut above the rest because of one quiet achiever: Teamwork Graph, a powerful data intelligence layer that connects all your Atlassian and third-party data. Unlike other data solutions that only aggregate data, Teamwork Graph makes sense of how everythingfrom your work to your knowledge, teams, and goalsis connected by mapping billions of data connections across over 10 billion data objects. If youve experienced Rovo, you know that its AI that really gets you. Teamwork Graph is why.

Soon, thanks to Teamwork Graph, Rovo will even anticipate your workflow needs, offering context-aware, action-oriented recommendations that enhance productivity and increase adoption. It complements how your teams already use Atlassian’s apps and seamlessly integrates into daily tasks so you can stay ahead of the curve, reduce cognitive overload, and know how to get the most out of collaborating with Rovo.

An AI transformation you can trust

Our customers have trusted us with many major transformations, from cloud to distributed work and so much in between. Your team’s AI transformation is no different, which is why we’ve obsessed over making sure you can trust Rovo.

Rovo is built responsibly on enterprise-grade infrastructure, featuring secure language models that meet our stringent security standards (including zero-day retention), strict permission enforcement and enhanced admin controls, and now Data Residency support (plus key certifications like SOC2 and ISO27001), so that admins are in control.

Soon, well even have an Atlassian-hosted LLM option that keeps your team as collaborative with AI as ever, all with your data safely in the Atlassian cloud.

Ready, set, Rovo!

Rovo is so much more than AI you can use to accelerate workit’s a strategic AI partnership that sits at the very center of the most high-impact and rewarding teamwork. Learn more about how all you Jira, Confluence, and Jira Service Management customers can get your hands on Rovo through Premium, Enterprise, and Standard subscriptions here.

As part of Rovo for all, were rolling out usage quotas of AI credits and Indexed Objects in our plans and collections. Weve designed our quotas thoughtfully so that all customers can experience Rovo’s many benefits.

We can’t wait to see how you and Rovo transform teamwork together!

Rovo for all launched at our annual user conference, Team ’25, alongside a number of exciting announcements. Before you fall into a FOMO spiral, check out our live-streamed and on-demand sessions.
